Tech giant launches G9198 flip phone two touchscreens

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 29, 2024

The G9198 features two 3.9-inch Super Amoled touchscreens, on the inside and the outside of the top half of the handset, both featuring a WXGA (768x1280 pixels) resolution. It runs on Android; however, the official listing does not specify what version. It also offers dual-SIM support and comes with 4G LTE, 3G, and 2G connectivity options. The website listing reveals that the handset supports China Mobile or China Unicom 4G networks.

The Samsung SM-G9198 is powered by a hexa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 808 processor, clocking in at 1.8GHz with 2GB of RAM power. The flip phone sports a 16-megapixel rear camera with LED flash, while there is a 5-megapixel front-facing camera also on board. The camera sensors on the Samsung G9198 are expected to pack the same sensors as the Samsung Galaxy S6, Galaxy S6 Edge, and Galaxy Note 5.

The phone has 16GB of inbuilt storage and it supports expandable storage up to 128GB (via microSD card). Other features include Bluetooth support, Wi-Fi, GPS, GLONASS, and Micro-USB connectivity options as well as a 2020mAh battery. It measures 120.3x61.2x16.3mm and weighs 204 grams. It also features a T9 keyboard.
