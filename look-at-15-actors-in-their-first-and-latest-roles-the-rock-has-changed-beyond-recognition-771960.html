Look at 15 Actors in Their First and Latest Roles (The Rock Has Changed Beyond Recognition)

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 13, 2024

When we look at our favorite actors, it seems that they just don’t change over the years. But unfortunately, (or fortunately), they do. When we see Charlize Theron or Salma Hayek, we realize that year after year, their appearance becomes even more interesting. Don’t you agree?

We at Bright Side are looking forward to seeing the next roles of the actors in this compilation and now you can see their cool characters again.

Keanu Reeves: Youngblood (1986) — John Wick: Chapter 3 — Parabellum (2019)

Kate Beckinsale: Devices and Desires (1991) — The Widow (2019)

Jared Leto: Camp Wilder (1992–1993) — The Outside (2017)

Ryan Gosling: Goosebumps, (1995–1998) — First Man (2018)
