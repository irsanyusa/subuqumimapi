Rita Wilson (Actress and Singer)

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, May 20, 2024

Full Name: Margarita Ibrahimoff
Profession: Actress and Singer

Biography: Rita Wilson is an American actress, producer, and singer best known for her roles in film and television. She is married to actor Tom Hanks and has survived breast cancer, which has influenced her philanthropic efforts, particularly in cancer research.

Wilson began her acting career with a guest appearance on The Brady Bunch in 1972. Following her studies at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, she went on to feature in films such as Sleepless in Seattle (1993) and Runaway Bride (1999). She also took roles on television shows like The Good Wife and appeared on Broadway.

As a producer, Wilson achieved success with My Big Fat Greek Wedding (2002) and was involved in the Mamma Mia! film series. She launched her production company, Artistic Films, with A Man Called Otto (2022).

In her music career, Rita Wilson released her debut album AM/FM in 2012 and followed up with several more, including Halfway to Home (2019). Her music has featured in films and she has performed at significant events, including once for President Barack Obama.

Married to Tom Hanks, with whom she shares two children, Chet and Truman, Wilson's personal life came into the spotlight when she was diagnosed with breast cancer. Following a double mastectomy and reconstructive surgery in 2015, she became an advocate for cancer awareness.

In further philanthropy, Wilson and Hanks received honorary naturalization in Greece for their relief efforts after the Mati Fire. Wilson was honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in March 2019 and a commemorative stamp by the Greek Postal Service ELTA in 2016.

In her recent work, Wilson produced and worked on the soundtrack for A Man Called Otto (2022). She released Now & Forever (2022), a duets album, and performed at the White House in 2023.
