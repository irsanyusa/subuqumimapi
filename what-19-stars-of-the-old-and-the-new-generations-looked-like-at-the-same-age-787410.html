What 19 Stars of the Old and the New Generations Looked Like at the Same Age

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, June 22, 2024

Experts estimate that by the year 2025, the global beauty and personal care product market size will reach $716.6 billion. Celebrities are the people who probably take the most care of their appearance, almost more than anyone else. They constantly use the services of beauty industry experts and they try to be on the same page with all the latest fashion trends.

We at Bright Side put together some examples of celebrities from the previous century and the modern time to see how the concept of beauty has changed, which beauty tricks were popular in the past, and which ones are popular now.

Britney Spears and Billie Eilish, 17 years old

Angelina Jolie and Jennifer Lawrence, 20 years old

Catherine Zeta-Jones and Emily Ratajkowski, 22 years old

Michelle Pfeiffer and Chloë Grace Moretz, 22 years old

Courteney Cox and Megan Fox, 23 years old

Drew Barrymore and Saoirse Ronan, 23 years old

Julia Roberts and Selena Gomez, 24 years old

Kate Winslet and Margot Robbie, 25 years old

Madonna and Miley Cyrus, 26 years old

Victoria Beckham and Ariana Grande, 26 years old

Melanie Griffith and Amber Heard, 31 years old

Brigitte Bardot and Scarlett Johansson, 31 years old

Nicole Kidman and Emily Blunt, 34 years old

Bonnie Tyler and Avril Lavigne, 35 years old

Emma Thompson and Sienna Miller, 37 years old

Kirstie Alley and Fergie, 39 years old

Glenn Close and Malin Åkerman, 40 years old

Kathy Bates and Mayim Bialik, 43 years old

Joan Collins and Winona Ryder, 47 years old

What modern beauty industry trends do you like the most? And what trends of the past would you like to see again in the fashion of today?
