Ariana Grande Shares Behind The Scenes Clips of Her Producing, Recording &quot;Yes, And?&quot;

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 19, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Bruce Glikas/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Ariana Grande recently gave fans an inside look at the production of her new single “Yes, And?” by posting behind-the-scenes videos on Instagram. The pop star shared clips of herself in the recording studio laying down vocals for the track, as well as producing elements of the song.

Ariana Grande Posts Videos From The Recording Studio

In one video, Grande is seen in the booth recording takes of the chorus for “Yes, And?” singing the empowering lines. In the background, the legendary producer, Max Martin gives feedback on Grande’s performance. He specifically compliments her “perfect diction” on the verse.

The brief but fascinating clip provides a glimpse into Grande’s artistic process and shows her powerful vocals and professionalism in the studio. Fans were excited to see this behind-the-scenes look at how Grande brings her music to life.

In addition to recording, Grande also gave fans a peek at her working behind the mixing boards, producing “Yes, And?” In a series of Instagram stories, Grande shared videos of herself tweaking elements of the track on ProTools.

“Doing my favorite thing….producing,” Grande captioned one video that shows her solo in the studio late at night focused intently on crafting the sound of “Yes, And?.”

Other clips show Grande discussing the production choices for the song with her team. “Should we keep the pre-chorus stripped back and let the chorus hit harder?” she asks.

Critics are raving about Ariana Grande’s “Yes, And?,” calling it a bold, innovative pop song. This praise is a clear validation of her hands-on approach to producing her own music. Fans loved seeing Grande flex her skills as both a powerhouse vocalist and a savvy producer.

Grande Thanks Fans for Embracing “Yes, And?”

On the heels of sharing these exclusive behind-the-scenes videos, Grande took to Instagram to thank fans for their overwhelming support of “Yes, And?“

“Thank you for the overwhelming amount of love and for dancing along. My heart is so full, and I’m so moved. Love you all so so so much,” she wrote gratefully.

Indeed, “Yes, And?” has quickly become a fan-favorite singles from Grande’s upcoming seventh studio album, which is due out later this year. It shot to #1 on iTunes in over 90 countries upon release.

The empowering dance-pop anthem showcases Grande’s stunning voice and production skills. Getting a glimpse of Grande’s hard work producing and recording the track via these new videos makes fans appreciate “Yes, And?” even more. They are thankful that Grande gave them special access to her creative process.
