America’s Got Talent alum Sofie Dossi has taken on yet another path in her career. We’ve seen her as a contortionist and a YouTuber, but now she’s attempting to break into the music industry. Dossi released her debut single on August 18.

America’s Got Talent fan favorite Sofie Dossi has finally joined the music industry. For those who may not remember, Dossi appeared on Season 11 of America’s Got Talent as a self taught contortionist. After finishing her original season out in the Top 10, she returned to the stage for America’s Got Talent: The Champions. Since then, she’s returned to the stage as a guest performer.

Sofie Dossi Premieres Break Up Anthem “Bunny”

Fans of the contortionist are excited to see her branch out to music. Along with the release of her debut single, she dropped a fast paced music video. Since the song is about a break up, Dossi is seen destroying stuffed animals, admiring a knife, and more. This video is captivating, since the storyline follows the song perfectly. It begins with Dossi in her bedroom destroying stuffed animals, leads to her going out with friends, then on to the darker storyline featuring a terrifying snake.

“Bunny” is a strong debut single for the AGT contortionist. We’ve previously seen clips of her singing, but nothing like this. We’re thrilled for Dossi in the next chapter of her career. Although this song has not landed on any charts just yet, it has brought in over 263,000 views on YouTube. The preview for the video has gained over 181,800 views on TikTok and over 52,000 likes on Instagram.

Dossi still leads a successful career following her time on the competition series. She currently has seven million subscribers on YouTube, 4.4 million followers on Instagram, and 11.1 million followers on TikTok.
