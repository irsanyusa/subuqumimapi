Rapper reacts to Blac Chyna's new tattoo

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 15, 2024

According to sources close to the rapper, Future is not the least bit worried about the model going ahead to tattoo his name on her hand just a month after they began dating.

Sources close to the couple say Chyna's tatt proves they have a real connection and  Future sees it as a very real, very permanent gesture of commitment ... and while fans are just getting used to it, the ink isn't even new.

Chyna actually got it inked on a whim, while Future was getting his own tatt (NOT her name) two and a half weeks ago, TMZ reports.

The source says Chyna has zero regrets and lives by the motto: "It's my body and I can do what I want with it,"  and she could use a laser removal, in case they aren't together forever.
